Hello, my name is Marcos Cipriano. founder of Sharp, a creative tech studio based in Los Angeles. I’m a full stack developer with a background in front-end design and a passion for building bold, intelligent digital experiences. At Sharp, I bring together clean design, smart code, and automation systems to help businesses move faster and look better while doing it.

Originally from Brazil with Italian roots, I’ve lived and worked across the globe before finding home in LA. I’m an artist at heart with a mind wired for technology — blending visuals, interfaces, and systems into work that’s not just beautiful, but built to perform.

You might know me online as @ciprianoworld that’s my creative identity, where the art side of my brain leads. It’s where ideas turn into visuals, and inspiration becomes form — from 3D design and motion graphics to branding and visual storytelling.

My skill set spans branding, UX/UI, 3D modeling, app development, and creative automation. I collaborate with visionaries, founders, and teams to bring ideas to life — from first sketch to fully working product.
