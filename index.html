Hello, my name is Marcos Cipriano. well known on social media as @ciprianoworld I’m the founder of Sharp a creative and development studio based in Los Angeles, where I transform ideas out of the paper. I’m passionate about making creativity come to life.

Originally from Brazil, with Italian roots, I’ve lived and learned across the globe, ultimately finding a home in Los Angeles. I’m happily married to the love of my life, and together we share our home with one dingleberry cat.

I consider myself an artist at heart with a mind for technology. My background spans web and app development, 3D modeling, graphic and label design, and marketing, giving me a unique perspective on blending the worlds of tech and art.
